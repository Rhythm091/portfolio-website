About Me

Entrepreneur. Builder. Educator.

I founded Dream Build IT Solutions at 15 — not as a school project, but because I saw real gaps in Nepal's digital landscape and decided to close them. Today I lead a team working across cybersecurity, AI, healthcare tech, and robotics.

I've reported security vulnerabilities to national organizations and multinational companies, organized Nepal's first youth-led hackathon, represented my country at IIT Patna, and built safety devices for women and the elderly — all before finishing high school.

My goal is simple: use technology to solve real problems for real people, starting in Nepal and scaling globally.
